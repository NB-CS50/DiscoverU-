# APEX

## Overview

APEX is a web-based platform designed to help university students better understand and utilize the features of email platforms such as Microsoft Outlook.

Many students receive large volumes of academic emails and often miss important information because they are unfamiliar with tools that can help them organize, prioritize, and manage their inboxes effectively.

APEX addresses this problem through structured learning resources and interactive gamified learning experiences.

---

## Problem Statement

University students often:

- Miss important announcements and deadlines.
- Feel overwhelmed by the volume of emails they receive.
- Underutilize powerful email features available in Outlook and similar platforms.
- Depend on friends, WhatsApp groups, or repeated reminders to stay informed.

---

## Solution

APEX provides:

- Easy-to-understand email feature guides.
- Interactive learning experiences.
- Gamified challenges to reinforce learning.
- Progress tracking for users.
- Centralized educational content about email productivity.
