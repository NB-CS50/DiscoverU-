The Challenge

Missing out because of email overload?

You're not alone. Here's what students face — and how DiscoverU fixes it.

⚠️ The Problem

Opportunities slipping through the cracks

University students — especially first years — struggle to track new opportunities like external events, school activities, and internships. Multiple emails flood in daily, and students end up relying on unreliable group chats to catch what they missed.

✅ The Solution

AI-powered filtering built around you

The DiscoverU app provides university students with customisable AI tools to sort through and filter emails tailored to each user's preferences — so the right information reaches you first, without the noise.

How It Works

Up and running in three steps

1

Connect your email

Log in to your university email account through the DiscoverU app — quick and secure.

2

Set your preferences

Choose which types of emails matter most — events, academic notices, internships — and we'll prioritise accordingly.

3

Get a clear summary

Receive your most important emails in a clean, structured format. No more inbox chaos.
